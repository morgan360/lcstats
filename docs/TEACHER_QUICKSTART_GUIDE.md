## ğŸ‘¥ Getting Your Students Set Up

### Student Registration Process

Your students will register themselves using a **registration code** that you'll receive separately. Here's how it works:

**What you need to do:**
- Share the registration code with your students (via email, classroom announcement, etc.)
- Students will be **automatically added to your class** when they register with this code

**What students need to do:**
1. Go to **https://www.numscoil.ie**
2. Click **"Sign Up"**
3. Fill in their details:
   - Username (they choose)
   - Email address
   - Password
   - **Registration Code** (the one you provide)
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. They're automatically enrolled in your class!

**Important Notes:**
- Each registration code can be used by multiple students (unless your admin sets a limit)
- Students are immediately added to your class - no manual enrollment needed
- Students can start using NumScoil right away after registration
