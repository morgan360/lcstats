About LCAI Maths

Empowering Leaving Certificate students with AI-powered mathematics practice and personalized learning.

Our Mission

LCAI Maths was created to help Leaving Certificate students master Honours Mathematics through interactive, AI-powered practice. We believe that every student deserves access to high-quality, personalized learning tools that adapt to their individual needs.

By combining comprehensive question banks with intelligent feedback systems, we're making it easier for students to practice, learn from mistakes, and build confidence in their mathematical abilities.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Question Bank

Access over 112 practice questions covering all major topics in the Leaving Certificate Honours Maths curriculum, including Probability, Statistics, Complex Numbers, Algebra, Calculus, and more.

AI-Powered Feedback

Our advanced AI system analyzes your answers and provides detailed, contextual feedback. Get instant explanations, identify where you went wrong, and learn the correct approach to solving problems.

Smart Hints & Solutions

Stuck on a question? Request intelligent hints that guide you toward the solution without giving away the answer. Access full step-by-step solutions when you need to understand the complete method.

We are educators and technologists passionate about improving mathematics education. Our team combines deep expertise in Leaving Certificate curriculum requirements with advanced AI and software development skills to create an effective, user-friendly learning platform.
