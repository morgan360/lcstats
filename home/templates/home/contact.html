Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a registration code?

Registration codes are provided by teachers to their students. If you're a student, ask your maths teacher for a code. If you're a teacher interested in using LCAI Maths, contact us at teachers@lcaimaths.ie.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Please contact your teacher or email us at support@lcaimaths.ie with your username, and we'll help you reset your password.

Can I use LCAI Maths on my phone or tablet?

Yes! LCAI Maths works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. However, for the best experience with mathematical input, we recommend using a desktop or tablet with a keyboard.

How does the AI marking work?

Our AI system analyzes your mathematical answers and compares them to the correct solution, understanding different approaches and formats. It provides instant feedback, explanations, and suggestions for improvement.

Is my progress saved?

Yes! All your question attempts, scores, and progress are automatically saved to your account. You can review your performance anytime from your dashboard.
